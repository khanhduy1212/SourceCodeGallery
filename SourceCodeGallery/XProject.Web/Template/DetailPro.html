A spring sojourn

on the Amalfi Coast.

After a long and dreary winter, treat yourself to a Spring stay on the Amalfi Coast to celebrate new beginnings, longer lights and enjoy the sweet air of spring. The offer is valid for stays of minimum 2 nights from 7th April 2019 to 15th May 2019 and it entitles to: 20% off our best available rates, an EUR 80,00 credit voucher that can be used to offset against any hotel Food & Beverage or SPA incidental charges (excluding mini-bar). Guests who purchase this offer will also be able to avail of either early check-in or late check-out (upon availability) and upgrade (subject to availability).

Offer is valid for stays between April 7th 2019 and May 15th 2019.
Offer is applicable to stays of minimum 2 nights in all room types.
Pre-payment is requested upon confirmation and it is non-refundable in case of cancellation.
This offer is not retroactive and cannot be combined with other existing promotions.
